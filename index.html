Founded in 2021, TBChess is a friendly community of board game enthusiasts and chess players who meet weekly in St. Petersburg, FL. Whether you're a beginner or a grandmaster, join us for exciting matches, strategy discussions, and fun events!

Location

Chess in St. Petersburg, FL

Chess has a deep and storied history in St. Petersburg, FL, with local clubs, tournaments, and friendly players shaping the city’s chess culture for decades. From casual games in parks and coffee shops to highly competitive events, St. Petersburg has long been a gathering place for those who appreciate the strategy, skill, and camaraderie that chess offers. The city has hosted numerous tournaments and fostered generations of players, creating a welcoming environment for both newcomers and seasoned competitors. TBChess proudly continues this tradition, serving as a dynamic hub for chess enthusiasts of all skill levels. Whether you’re a beginner eager to learn the fundamentals, a casual player looking to sharpen your skills, or a competitive expert seeking a challenge, TBChess provides a space where everyone can enjoy the game in a friendly and engaging atmosphere. Through weekly meetups, exciting events, and a strong sense of community, TBChess connects players who share a passion for the game, ensuring that St. Petersburg remains a vibrant center for chess enthusiasts for years to come.
