Our Tournament Software

We organize tournaments using our very own TB Chess App, which is a modern free open source platform we created for running engaging self-managed chess tournaments. Simply access the app, sign up for an event and start playing! The app organizes the rounds, calculates the standings, keeps track of players' ELOs all in real-time from your phone!

Are you a club or a person looking to host a tournament using our platform? Get in touch! We'd love to help out.

About TB Chess

TB Chess aims to offer the St. Pete chess community a place where chess events are FREE to everyone. We provide a space where everyone can enjoy the game in a friendly and engaging atmosphere. Our games are competitive, but we don't take ourselves too seriously. Through weekly meetups, exciting events, and a strong sense of community, we connect players who share a passion for the game.
